Personal Projects

These are just some of my personal projects. I'm slowly chipping away at them and hope to get them published some day!

Outsiders is a culmination of my love for superheroes and the found family trope. It follows the journey of Chandran Hegde, a man that finds himself in a world of superhumans and a hard place. Over the course of his journey, he goes through a lot of pain, but he also learns how to cope with it and find solace in those close to him. This story aims to show the more human side of superheroes and vigilantes, as well as being action-packed.

Love Bites

Love Bites is a story I never expected to write. It's a simple rom-com about Miguel and Lana, a simple fellow and his werewolf girlfriend. Their story is all about loving your other and being there for them emotionally, as they work through the several hijinx and pent-up emotions regarding Lana's lycanthropy. This story is a love-letter to my own fascination of werewolves and appreciation for healthy relationships.
